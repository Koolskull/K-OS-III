# Why Monero exists, and what it actually provides

*This lesson is a stub. See `EDUCATION.md` for the project's curriculum and `docs/SEED_ISSUES.md` for the issue to flesh this out.*

Monero is the most widely used cryptocurrency designed from the ground up for privacy of sender, receiver, and amount. This track is a companion for users who choose to operate in the Monero ecosystem and want to do so correctly — most users do not, and that's fine.

The track is honest about limits. Monero's privacy properties depend on the user's behavior at the boundary (deposits and withdrawals to KYC exchanges, network-level fingerprinting, transaction-graph analysis when funds re-enter the surveilled financial system). Holding XMR is not the same as transacting privately.
