# How Bitcoin thinks about money

*This lesson is a stub. See `EDUCATION.md` for the project's curriculum and `docs/SEED_ISSUES.md` for the issue to flesh this out.*

Bitcoin is older than Ethereum, smaller in scope, and built on a fundamentally different model of state. This track is short by design — most K-OS users will not need to write Bitcoin software, but many will need to understand what their Bitcoin wallet is actually doing.

Topics covered:

- The UTXO model and why "balance" is a derived concept
- Bitcoin Script basics
- Wallet types — single-sig, multisig, watch-only
- Lightning at a high level
